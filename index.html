About Me

I was born on November 20, 1990 in San Salvador, El Salvador. My family moved to the United States because of our country's civil war. I was seven years old when I first arrived. I consider the USA my home and have come to love living here.

I received an Associate's Degree in Engineering Science from Wilbur Wright College in 2011. I co-owned a small business for over 4 years specializing in repairing consumer electronics such as smartphones, computers and tablets. Now I am on a new career search which is why I enrolled in this coding bootcamp.

During my free time, I like to ride my motorcycle, play basketball with my nephew, skateboard and fiddle with electronics. I also love traveling and attending concerts!
